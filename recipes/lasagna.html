Lasagna Recipe

Here you will learn to make a delicious vegetarian lasagna that can be easily converted to vegan if required

Ingredients
For the mince
  • 500g Quorn Mince
  • 250ml Red Wine
  • 1 Medium size onion finely diced
  • 4 Garlic Cloves finely chopped
  • 1 Carrot finely diced
  • 1 Celery Stic finely dicedk
  • 2 Bay leaves
  • 1 Tin of chopped tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Olive oil
For the white sauce
  • 50g Butter
  • 50g Plain white flour
  • 500ml Milk
  • 1 Small onion
  • 2 Bay leaves
  • 6 Pepper corns
Method
  1. Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a frying pan and sweat the onion, carrot and celery for about 10 mins or until translucent all over.
  2. Add the garlic and cook for a further 2 minutes
  3. Add the mince and cook until browned
  4. Add the wine and reduce
  5. Add the tinned tomatoes and bay leaves and cook on a low heat until the mixture has reduced add salt and pepper to taste
  6. Warm the milk in a saucepan with the onion, bay leaves and pepper corns for 10 to 15 minutes. Do not boil this.
  7. In a separate pan, melt the butter and add the flour
  8. Cook until it has the consistency of wet sand
  9. Slowly add the warm milk and stir constantly
  10. Gradually add all of the milk (bit by bit)

    11. DO NOT ADD ALL OF THE MILK AT ONCE

  11. Once done it's time to layer your lasagne how you want
    12. I like to do a layer of mince, followed by a layer of lasagna then another of mince and lasagna. Finally I add all of the white sauce on top.
  12. Bake in a preheated oven at 200°C for 35 to 40 minutes
