My Satay Sauce

Here you will see my own receipe for a satay sauce.

Serve with grilled meats or vegetables, or make a little loose and use as a stir fry sauce

Ingredients
  • Peanut butter
  • Neutral oil e.g. sunflower
  • Small onion, chopped
  • 1 inch of Ginger grated
  • 1 red chilli
  • 4 Garlic Cloves finely chopped
  • 3 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 100 to 200ml boiling water
  • Grated nutmeg
Method
  1. Heat the oil in a saucepan and add the onion. Cook until begining to brown
  2. Add the garlic, ginger and chilli and cook for another 2 minutes
  3. Add the Peanut Butter and mix well
  4. Add the soy sauce and stir in
  5. Loosen to the right consistency with the boiled water
  6. Stir in the nutmeg to finish.
